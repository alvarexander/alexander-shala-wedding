Questions & Answers

We want you to enjoy our special day with as little stress as possible, so we’ve put together answers to some questions we thought you might have!

What's The Guest List?

As we planned our wedding, we knew we wanted something small, intimate, and truly meaningful to us. With a limited budget and venue capacity, we had to make some really difficult decisions about the guest list. If you didn’t receive an invitation, please know it’s absolutely not a reflection of how much we love or value you. This choice was about creating a space that felt right for us, not about excluding anyone. We are so grateful for your love, your support, and your presence in our lives—whether near or far. Your good wishes mean the world to us, and we can’t wait to celebrate together in other beautiful ways, soon.

What Is The Dress Code?

What matters most to us is that you’re there to celebrate with us. If you can, we kindly ask that you aim for business casual—think slacks and a button-up, or a nice dress. That said, we completely understand that not everyone has these items on hand, and we absolutely don’t want anyone to feel pressured to buy something new. Your presence is truly the most important thing.

What Should I Bring?

Just bring yourself and your good vibes! We’ll have food, drinks, and everything else covered—just come ready to celebrate with us!

Can I Take Photos & Videos?

We’d love for you to take photos and videos during the ceremony and reception! We just kindly ask that you do so in a way that doesn’t distract from the moment—please keep phones on silent and avoid using flash. We’ll also be sharing an open, free to join, online photo album on Google Photos so everyone can upload and enjoy each other’s memories. We can’t wait to see the day through your eyes! To join the album see: Photo Album

Can I Bring A Date or A Plus One?

We are so excited to celebrate with you! However, due to budget and venue limitations, we are unable to accommodate additional guests. We are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. Thank you for your understanding!
